Quick guide

  • Arithmetic: type numbers and use + − × ÷ = like any calculator.
  • TVM: enter a value then press N, I/Y, PV, PMT or FV to store it. Press CPT then a TVM key to solve for it.
  • Sign convention: money out is negative, money in is positive (e.g. a loan received is +PV, payments are −PMT).
  • 2ND: press 2ND then a key for the small label above it — e.g. 2ND · PMT toggles BGN/END, 2ND · I/Y sets P/Y & C/Y, 2ND · . sets decimal places, 2ND · FV clears the TVM registers.
  • Worksheets: use the tabs above the keypad (or the CF/NPV/IRR keys and 2ND · PV / 9 / 6 / 8) for Cash Flow, Amortization, Bond, Depreciation and Statistics.
  • Keyboard: digits, + - * / = ., Enter, Backspace, Esc (clear/close), c (CPT), Shift (2ND), n (±).
